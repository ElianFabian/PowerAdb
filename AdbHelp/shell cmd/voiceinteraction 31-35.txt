VoiceInteraction Service (voiceinteraction) commands:
  help
    Prints this help text.

  show
    Shows a session for the active service

  hide
    Hides the current session

  disable [true|false]
    Temporarily disable (when true) service

  restart-detection
    Force a restart of a hotword detection service

  set-debug-hotword-logging [true|false]
    Temporarily enable or disable debug logging for hotword result.
    The debug logging will be reset after one hour from last enable.
