allows you to create/modify/delete displays besides the default android display, as an example,
'multidisplay add 1 1200 800 240 0' will create/modify display 1 with dimension 1200x800,
 dpi 240 and the default flag
'multidisplay del 1' will delete the display 1

available sub-commands:
    add              add new or modify existing display
    del              remove existing display
